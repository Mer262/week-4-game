Crystals Collector

How to Play

Each of the 4 crystals have an unknown value. As you click on the crystals, a specific number of points will be added to the number in the "Your Current Score" box. Your goal is to NOT exceed the number displayed in the "Number to Match!" box. You win if your total score is equal to that number. If you exceed this number, you lose! Each time you play, the values of the crystals reset.
